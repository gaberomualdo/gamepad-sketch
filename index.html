Connect and Activate a Gamepad to Get Started

You may have click a couple of gamepad buttons for the gamepad to be recognized.

GamepadSketch

  • lt Draw
  • rt Force Perpendicular
  • lb + rb Clear
  • x Change Color
  • y Change Thickness
  • menu Download Drawing

Change Color

  • a Select Color
  • b Go Back

Change Thickness

  • a Select Thickness
  • b Go Back
